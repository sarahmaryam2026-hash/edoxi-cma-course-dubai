## What is CMA Certification?

The **Certified Management Accountant (CMA)** is a globally recognized professional certification that validates expertise in management accounting, financial planning, analysis, and strategic decision-making.

The CMA curriculum covers key areas such as:

- Financial Planning and Analysis
- Performance Management
- Cost Management
- Decision Analysis
- Risk Management
- Internal Controls
- Professional Ethics
- Corporate Governance
- Technology and Analytics

A CMA certification demonstrates your ability to contribute to business strategy and financial leadership beyond traditional accounting functions.

# How to Become a CMA: Step-by-Step Guide

## Step 1: Begin CMA Preparation

Enroll in Edoxi's CMA training program and start preparing through:

- Live instructor-led classes
- Practice tests
- Mock examinations
- Comprehensive study resources

## Step 2: Register with IMA

Become a member of the Institute of Management Accountants (IMA) and complete the CMA entrance requirements.

Edoxi provides guidance throughout the registration process.

## Step 3: Schedule Your Exams

The CMA exam consists of two parts.

Choose your preferred testing window and schedule your exam through an authorized Prometric Testing Center.

## Step 4: Pass Both Exam Parts

Successfully complete:

- CMA Part 1: Financial Planning, Performance, and Analytics
- CMA Part 2: Strategic Financial Management

Once completed, apply for your official CMA certification through the IMA.
